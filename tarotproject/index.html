Tarot 3-card reading

What is Tarot? Simply defined, Tarot cards are decks of cards with pictures, symbols, and numbers printed on them. In theory, tarot cards can be used for divination and can give insight into your past, present and future.


Tarot reading may seem supernatural and intimitdating to approach. However, Tarot cards can be quite simple and offer a different perspective to a difficult situation or question you may have. Additionally, reading Tarot cards can be simply a fun hobby.


Are you ready to start the Tarot reading? Let's begin!


Firstly, think of a question you would like answered from this reading.

  • Question should be simple and open.
  • Questions should help you to understand areas of uncertainty in your life.
  • Try to pick a topic that you do no have any bias towards
